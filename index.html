The Hudacks

We're Mike and Caroline Hudack. We met in New York City more than a decade ago. We live in rural Cambridgeshire with our daughters Marla and Ada, a family of ducks, a clan of badgers, some Muntjac deer and assorted other wildlife. We try to grow as much of our own food as possible.

We've been building and marketing things on the internet for a long time. We also angel invest in new companies in the US and UK. We only invest where we believe we can both add value.

Mike is building Sling Money, which he founded with some folks from Monzo Bank, where he was Chief Product Officer.

Mike's also on the board of VidIQ. Before Monzo he was Chief Product and Technology Officer at Deliveroo, the food delivery company. Mike was also, until recently, on the board of BookTrust, the children's reading charity.

Prior to Deliveroo Mike was at Facebook in California, where he worked on Identity and then ran Ads Product immediately after the IPO. After Ads he moved to London and ran Sharing Product.

Mike started an internet video company called Blip in 2005. Blip is now part of the Walt Disney Company. He's a high school dropout who got his start programming on his uncle's discarded IBM XT and his brother's Amiga 3000.

Caroline is a board member at Techspace and was previously Chief Operating Officer at Impala.

Before Impala Caroline was EMEA Marketing Director for Airbnb and EMEA Marketing Director for Facebook, where she was the first consumer marketer in Europe. Before moving to London Caroline was Product Marketing Manager for News Feed in Menlo Park.

Caroline started her career as a WPP fellow, working at a number of WPP's marketing agencies around the world. She was also a founding member of WPP Digital.

The Hudacks

We're Mike and Caroline Hudack. We live in a village in rural Cambridgeshire together with our two kids, a family of ducks, a clan of badgers and some Muntjac deer.

Mike is building something new at Avian Labs. He's also a non-executive board member at VidIQ and a trustee at BookTrust, the UK's largest children's reading charity.

He was previously Chief Product Officer at Monzo Bank. Before Monzo Mike was Chief Product & Technology Officer at Deliveroo. Prior to Deliveroo he was at Facebook where he worked on Identity and then ran Ads Product immediately after the IPO. After Ads he ran Sharing Product.

Mike started an internet video company called Blip in 2005. Blip is now part of the Walt Disney Company. He's a high school dropout and started out as a programmer and sysadmin.

Caroline is Chief Marketing Officer at Impala.

Before Impala Caroline was EMEA Marketing Director for Airbnb and EMEA Marketing Director for Facebook, where she was the first consumer marketer in Europe. Previous to moving to London Caroline was Product Marketing Manager for News Feed in Menlo Park.

Caroline started her career as a WPP fellow, working at a number of WPP's marketing agencies around the world. She was also a founding member of WPP Digital.
